Community Learninge Center Coverage

The map below shows the coverage of each community learning center in Sierra Leone in the form of a voronoi tessellation. Each dot, one per polygon, represents the location of a center, while the color represents its building type. An out of school youth located within the region contained in a polygon is closer to its community learning center than any other. The color of each polygon shows the population size. Visualization created using the geovoronoi.

* This visualization was created in cooperation with the Non-Formal Education at the Ministry of Basic and Senior Secondary Education and UNFPA.
* Catchment figures displayed here are projections based on limited inputs, are not official, and should not be considered dependable.
* Visualizations below are served through iframes and may not be accessible from UN internet connections.
